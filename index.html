Why We Exist

Every day, countless women and children suffer in silence. Rise Up! is a movement to expose abuse, empower survivors, and connect them with help.

Did You Know?

1 in 3

South African women experience domestic violence in their lifetime.

Why This Matters

In South Africa, over 50% of women have experienced gender-based violence. Many never speak out. Rise Up! exists to break the silence, spread awareness, and offer a lifeline to those in danger.
