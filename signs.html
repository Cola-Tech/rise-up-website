Warning Signs

Recognize the red flags — for yourself or someone you care about.

Deciding what you wear, where you go, who you speak to, or tracking your location without consent.

Cutting you off from family, friends, support systems, or activities that bring you joy or independence.

Shouting, name-calling, constant criticism, or gaslighting to erode your confidence and self-worth.

Restricting access to your own money, not allowing you to work, or making all financial decisions unilaterally.

Threats of harm, destruction of property, or using fear to keep control over you or your children.
